Our Facilities

Sea View Golf Course

One of three championship courses with stunning ocean views.

This championship course offers challenging holes with breathtaking ocean views, designed for players of all skill levels. Amenities include GPS carts, practice greens, and clubhouse facilities.

Two Clubhouses

Relax and socialize in our modern clubhouses.

Our clubhouses feature comfortable lounges, locker rooms, and dining areas perfect for relaxation before or after your game.

Hotel Accommodation

Luxury suites and rooms for a comfortable stay.

Enjoy our luxury suites with stunning views, modern amenities, and exceptional service to make your stay memorable.

Restaurant & Cafe

Enjoy gourmet dining and casual meals.

Our restaurant offers a diverse menu featuring local and international cuisine, perfect for any time of day.

Pro Shop

Find the latest golf equipment and apparel.

Stocked with the latest golf gear, apparel, and accessories to keep you at the top of your game.
